Welcome All Music Mix regulars, TT refugees, and new users! As always, our core rule is to be nice.

You can find us at plug.dj/everything-mix/

Our rules are pretty simple:

  • Please keep all videos safe for work *; audio does not need to be censored.
  • Please keep all songs under 8 minutes.
  • We expect you to treat others as you expect to be treated (aka BE NICE).

* We made this rule since many people listen to this site during the day at work. We don't want to get anyone fired because there happens to be a song playing with nudity or sexually explicit content. Please be considerate of this. However, I would hope they are using headphones at work, so the music itself can be as vulgar as you want. :-)
